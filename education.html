Relevant Classes

General Chemistry
This class taught the basics of chemistry and gave me a strong foundation for the more rigourous classes that would follow.
Organic Chemistry
This class focused on the molecules that make up life, such as oxygen, carbon, and hydrogen. We learned about many common reactions, molecular interactions, and the properties of the most fundamental molecules
Analytical Chemistry
This class and lab combination was more quantitative and taught me various methods to analyze chemical compounds and molecules.
Physical Chemistry
This class was very conceptual and was mainly about chemistry in relation to physics. There were many overlapping ideas that expanded my knowledge on the reasoning behind common chemical ideas.
Intro to Biochemistry
As the first class I took that was directly related to my major, this class was about the basics of biochemistry, but also the types of experiments that are typically conducted in the field.
Biochemical Methods
This class had a goal of making us think like scientists. We do experiments, but we also design them and try to understand how the experiments we do are answering the questions we want to know the answers to.
